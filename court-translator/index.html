How it works

Step 1 — Translation: Your Hindi text is sent to Sarvam AI's translation engine (sarvam-translate:v1, formal mode), which handles Indic language nuances far better than generic translators. Long texts are automatically split into chunks at paragraph and sentence boundaries.

Step 2 — Legal refinement: The raw translation is refined by an open-source LLM (Llama 3.3 70B via Groq) that applies correct Indian legal English — “Petitioner” not “applicant”, “Hon'ble” not “honourable”, “filed a petition” not “submitted an application”, “disposed of” not “resolved”. Case citations (AIR, SCC) and petition structure (Prayer, Grounds, Synopsis) are preserved.

Privacy: All processing happens server-side. Your text is sent to Sarvam AI and Groq for processing but is not stored.

Disclaimer

This is an AI-assisted translation tool. While it uses legal terminology refinement, the output should be reviewed by a qualified legal professional before use in any official filing.
