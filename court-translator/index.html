How it works

Document extraction: Text is extracted entirely in your browser. Digital PDFs use PDF.js. Scanned PDFs and images use Tesseract.js with Hindi (Devanagari) OCR. Word documents (.docx) use mammoth.js. No files are uploaded to any server for extraction.

Translation: Extracted Hindi text is sent to Sarvam AI (sarvam-translate:v1, formal mode) for translation, then refined by Llama 3.3 70B (via Groq) with a legal-terminology-aware prompt — “Petitioner” not “applicant”, “Hon'ble” not “honourable”, “disposed of” not “resolved”, etc.

Disclaimer
