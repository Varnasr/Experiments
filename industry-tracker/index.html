About this project

This database compiles publicly-available information on major infrastructure projects associated with the Adani Group and Reliance / Ambani Group between 2001 and 2025. The intent is to give journalists, researchers, civil-society organisations, and curious citizens a single place to see how these conglomerates' footprints have grown across sectors, states, and political administrations.

  • Sources: company filings, MoEFCC clearances, court documents, Parliament Q&A, RTI responses, audited media reporting.
  • Government-support classification covers explicit subsidies, land allotments, regulatory exemptions, sovereign loan guarantees, and PLI scheme participation.
  • Limits: private-equity stakes and offshore holding-company structures are partial; updates may lag where filings are delayed.
  • Contributing: errata and additions welcome — see the GitHub repo linked in the footer.
