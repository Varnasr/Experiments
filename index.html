⚠️ Critical Deadlines

📊 Data Entry Deadlines:

  • Water quality data: 1 day after collection
  • Pond information: 3 days after adding pond
  • Master doc updates: 1 day after pond info entry
  • Disease events: Same day as occurrence

🔧 Equipment Requirements:

  • Calibration: Before first use after storage
  • Follow-up visits: 2 days after corrective actions
  • Water quality measurements: Within 2 days of scheduled date
  • Disinfection: Between every pond visit
