Disclaimer

This is a research database that maps publicly known infrastructure projects and their relationship to government contracts. It does not allege wrongdoing.

All data is sourced from public filings, news reports, and government records. "Government-linked" means the project involves public contracts, PPP agreements, or state resources — a standard feature of infrastructure development worldwide.

The term "crony capitalism" refers to an academic/journalistic framework for analyzing business-government relationships, not a legal determination.

Sources: Company annual reports, SEBI filings, government tender documents, Reuters, Bloomberg, investigative journalism reports.
